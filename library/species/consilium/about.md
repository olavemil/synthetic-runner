Consilium — five-persona deliberation with ghost synthesis.

Five named personas draft responses independently, then a structured pipeline reduces
candidates through summarization, merging, and transformation. A personality-free ghost
voice handles synthesis steps (drafting alternative one-liners, merging summaries).

Pipeline: 8 candidates (5 persona drafts + 3 ghost one-liners) → 4 reductions → 1 merged
spec → 1 transformed final reply. Each persona reviews the sent response with a one-line
analysis.

Heartbeat: per-persona thinking sessions with shared knowledge and creative spaces,
organized and archived for brevity.
